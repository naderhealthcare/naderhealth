About Nader Health & Life Advocacy and Consulting

Our Mission

Empowering individuals and families to navigate health and life’s most challenging events—always with clarity, confidence, and compassion.

Our Story

With more than 49 years in nursing and 46 years in healthcare leadership, Nader Health & Life Advocacy and Consulting was founded with a simple but powerful vision: to support people as they face the complexities of the healthcare system. Guided by deep clinical experience and an unwavering commitment to advocacy, we created a team dedicated to making healthcare clearer, kinder, and more accessible for every patient.

Our Team

Our advocates bring backgrounds in clinical management, patient-centered care, staff advocacy, and quality improvement. We’ve helped patients and families in hospitals, at home, and even internationally—always serving as educators, coordinators, and a calming voice in times of stress.

Our Values

  • Compassion in every interaction
  • Advocacy for every voice
  • Clarity and transparency in care
  • Empowerment through education

Our Impact

Whether supporting a patient through a new diagnosis or helping a family coordinate complex care, we are proud to stand by our clients—bringing expertise, integrity, and reassurance every step of the way. Let our experience be your guide to healing and peace of mind.
